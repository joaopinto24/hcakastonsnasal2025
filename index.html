Our Mission: Clean Up Space

We're dedicated to removing space debris and preserving the orbital environment for future generations. Our innovative technology and community-driven approach are making space safer for satellites, astronauts, and space exploration missions worldwide. We do this by batching debris removal missions and using our own satellites to deorbit and burn up the debris in the atmosphere.
